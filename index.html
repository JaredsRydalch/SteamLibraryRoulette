How to set your profile to public:

  1. Go to your Steam profile
  2. Click the Edit Profile button in the top right
  3. Select "My Privacy Settings" on the right
  4. Click the dropdown after My Profile:
  5. Click "Public" from the dropdown
  6. Click the dropdown after Game Details:
  7. Click "Public" from the dropdown

Additionally, your game details must be set to public, which can be done on the same page.
